Codewars: RGB to hex conversion - Clojure

A quickie to do while waiting for the company's daily meeting to start.

The Problem

You are given three decimal integers r, g, and b and are to create an upper case hexadeximal color string from them.

My solution

There is a number of ways for converting decimals to hex. If you want to practise your maths, you can do the base transformation by hand, you can use Integer/toHexString, but if you're familiar with them (or can't be bothered), the most concise way is to just use string formatting: (format "%02X" n), where %02 means "ensure two digits", and the format X is for "uppercase hexadeximal", in contrast to x, its lowercase sibling.

Bear in mind that the input may be outside the valid range [0, 255].

(defn clamp [lower upper n] (max lower (min upper n)))

(defn rgb [r g b]
  (apply format "%02X%02X%02X" (map (partial clamp 0 255) [r g b])))

I'm the first to admit that clamp is too general for that specific problem, as there is only one set of upper and lower limits. Making it take parametric limits came out of habit.

(defn clamp-hex [n] (max 0 (min 255 n)))

(defn rgb [r g b]
  (apply format "%02X%02X%02X" (map clamp-hex [r g b])))

Is a little less over-engineered.
